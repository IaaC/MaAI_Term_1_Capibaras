AI-Driven Pathfinding for Hospitals

Shortest & Most Efficient Routes for Visually Impaired Individuals

Key Features

Pathfinding

Our AI model analyzes the hospital layout (IFC or 3D files) to compute the shortest, most accessible path for visually impaired individuals.

Design Suggestions

Get expert design recommendations over extracted views from the 3D model or site images to enhance accessibility.

IFC or 3D Model Import

Architects can easily upload IFC or 3D models to see real-time path computations and suggestions for improvements.

How It Works

1. Upload Your Model

Provide your IFC or 3D model through our easy import interface.

2. AI Calculation

Our AI algorithms analyze the layout, simulate multiple paths, and identify the most efficient and accessible route.

3. Accessibility Recommendations

The system gives additional design tips on floor plans, lighting, signage, and any improvements that can further aid visually impaired individuals.

4. Download & Review

Export the path recommendations and design suggestions as comprehensive reports or interactive 3D overlays.
